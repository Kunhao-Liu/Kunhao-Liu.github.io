Kunhao Liu

I am a third-year Ph.D. student at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, specializing in 3D computer vision research under the guidance of Prof. Shijian Lu. I was also a visiting student at Max Planck Institute for Informatics with Prof. Christian Theobalt and Dr. Fangneng Zhan. I have a broad research interest in 3D computer vision, spanning rendering, generation, understanding, and manipulation.
